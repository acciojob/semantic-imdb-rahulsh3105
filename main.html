The Matrix is a 1999 science fiction action film starring Keanu Reeves, Laurence Fishburne, and Carrie-Anne Moss. The movie is set in a dystopian future, where humanity is trapped inside a simulated reality created by sentient machines to distract and pacify them, while their bodies are used as energy sources.
